### Research

* [Dynamic availability](https://ethresear.ch/t/why-ethereum-needs-a-dynamically-available-protocol/24418): proposal for next consensus protocol, two decoupled layers, heartbeat produced by small randomly-sampled committee & trailing finality gadget using full active validator set  
* [Binary tries optimal group depth](https://cperezz.github.io/bintrie-benchmarks/group-depth-benchmarks/index.html) benchmark: sweet spot is 5-6 bits  
* [Falcon signature aggregation for post quantum transaction pools](https://ethresear.ch/t/revisiting-falcon-signature-aggregation-for-pq-mempools/24431): key recovery without aggregation most straightforward path to deployment due to lower storage costs & simplicity  
* [Post-quantum threats to app layer privacy](https://ethresear.ch/t/post-quantum-threats-to-ethereum-privacy/24450), such as stealth addresses, zkID & zkTLS

### Regulation

* US SEC [securities laws application to crypto](https://www.sec.gov/newsroom/press-releases/2026-30-sec-clarifies-application-federal-securities-laws-crypto-assets)  
  * Token taxonomy: Not securities: digital commodities (e.g. ETH), digital collectibles, digital tools (e.g. ENS), stablecoins (GENIUS Act payment stablecoins); Securities: digital securities  
  * Non-securities transactions: protocol staking, wrapping & airdrops  
  * Investment contracts: how a non-security crypto asset becomes/ceases to be subject to an investment contract  
* US SEC: [Nasdaq tokenized securities trading approved](https://www.sec.gov/files/rules/sro/nasdaq/2026/34-105047.pdf) for pilot  
* US CFTC: [Phantom no action letter](https://www.cftc.gov/PressRoom/PressReleases/9197-26), allows self custodial wallet to connect users to regulated derivatives trading without broker registration
